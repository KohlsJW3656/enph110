Economic Impact of Wildfires

Damage and Loss to Property

  • In 2020, 18,000 structures in the US were destroyed, 54% of which were homes

  • In California in 2018, wildfires caused 28 billion dollars in capital losses

  • Homes in high-risk fire areas have extremely high insurance costs due to the risk of fires year-to-year

Economic Activity Impacts

  • Cause a disruption of normal economic activity

  • Destroying property, interrupting transportation, people's jobs, and decreasing tourism/recreation

  • Most of the losses caused by wildfires are caused by a disruption to economic activity

  • Displacement of hundreds of thousands of Americans each year
