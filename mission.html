Our Mission

Our Goal

Our goal is to spread awareness on the economic, enviornmental, and human health related effects from wildfires.

Why is this topic important?

This topic is important because clean air is important for healthy human development. Poor air quality also negatively affects people of all ages when doing things outside. Because of this, we should find ways to improve the quality of our air.
